Privacy Policy

The Nippon Foundation and Mirairo Inc. (called as "we", "us", or "our" as follows) hereby state about our handling of personal information that we have social responsibility to manage personal information appropriately and safely. We also establish our policy on the protection of personal information. All of our executives and employees shall follow this policy to protect personal information.

1. We shall specify our purposes for using personal information as possible and handle personal information appropriately within the scope necessary to achieve the purposes.

2. We shall endeavor to execute appropriate preventive and corrective measures and to ensure our safety management as well as to prevent the loss, leak or misuse, etc. in regards to our personal information management.

3. We shall acquire personal information legally and appropriately.

4. We shall observe every law related to personal information as well as our in-house regulations based on the privacy policy.

[Identification of Personal Information]

Personal information contains individual specified data of the person, such as name, address, date of birth, telephone number, email address, place of employment and other descriptions.

Even though a person may not be identified by the information itself, it may be referred to the other information and the combination may make the information personally identifiable. As a result, such information shall also be contained in the same category.

[Purpose of Use of Personal Information]

Our purposes of use in regards to personal information are as follows:

1. To provide our service to the users.

2. To do subscription, etc. for campaigns, questionnaires and monitors which we and our group companies carry out.

3. To inform about the products, service etc. which we and our group companies provide.

4. To answer inquiries concerning our service.

[Change of Purpose of Use of Personal Information]

In case we shall change the Purpose of Use of user's personal information, it shall be changed within a reasonable range which is relevant to the one before the change. Users shall be notified of the change if Purpose of Use is updated.

[Provision of Personal Information]

In principle, we shall not provide any personal information to a third party without obtaining the user’s consent.

We shall provide personal information upon specifying its receivers and the contents of the supplying information only when obtained the consent from the user.

However, in the following cases, we may disclose personal information without obtaining consent of the user.

1. If it is allowed by law.

2. If we shall be prompted for its disclosure from the court, the Public Prosecutor's Office, police, bar associations or other equivalent authorized organizations.

3. If we shall determine that a user may prevent a disadvantage to a third party.

[In Case Any Personal Information Is Not Provided]

We shall inform the users that the appropriate services may not be received if the users did not provide the personal information and input each required item to execute the services.

[Corrections of Personal Information]

We will swiftly make any correction to the personal information when users informed us by email.

[Outsourcing for Operation to Treat Personal Information]

We may outsource some or all of our operation to treat personal information, if necessary.

[Management of Personal Information]

For personal information which we manage, we shall endeavor to execute appropriate information security measures and to take safety measures against an unauthorized access or a virus infection, etc.in order to prevent the loss, leak or misuse, etc.

[Disclaimers]

We shall not take any responsibilities in the following cases:

1. When users disclose their personal information to a third party by usage of our provided functions or any other means.

2. When users can be identified by information which users disclose through our services by themselves.

[Preparation of Statistical Data]

In order to improve our service, we may prepare statistical data from which is based on the registered personal information and is treated so that the personal information may not be specified. Statistical data based on personal information shall be used without any limitation.

[Change of the Privacy Policy]

We shall change the contents of this privacy policy at any time.

In case any changes may take place, we shall announce it through this site.
